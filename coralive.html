与瑚共生

CO-LIVE意为共生，由Coral（珊瑚）和Live（生存）二者结合而来，包含作者对珊瑚与海洋生物多样性之关系的理解。在本件作品中，利用魔术贴把珊瑚图案变成一张可以自由创作的画布，意在鼓励每个人参与到海洋场景的想象与搭建中去。当各色各样的海洋图谱被呈现时，参与者们也就共同完成了本次设计的理念建构——CO-LIVE不仅仅指珊瑚与海洋，更是人类与珊瑚、人类与海洋的关系，而我们与珊瑚、与海洋可以是怎样的关系，则全由我们自己去定义。

COral-LIVE

In this work, the coral pattern is turned into a free canvas using Velcro, which is intended to encourage everyone to participate in the imagination and construction of marine scenes. When all kinds of ocean patterns are presented, the participants have completed the concept of this design together - CO-LIVE not only refers to coral and ocean, but also the relationship between human and coral, human and ocean, and it is up to us to define what kind of relationship we can have with coral and ocean.
