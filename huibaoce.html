研究生中期筛选汇报册

在广州美术学院就期间为「研究生中期筛选展」编辑并设计的手册，整理了本人研究生期间前半阶段的所学所思所感。在设计、选纸与装帧上，避免“正式感”，在展厅阅读时有如随手翻阅一本报纸，尽量以一种轻松舒适的方式对自身的学习进行回顾与记录。

MID-TERM REPORT FOR  MASTER DEGREE APPLICANTS

This booklet, edited and designed for the "Graduate Student Midterm Screening Exhibition" at the Guangzhou Academy of Fine Arts, is a compilation of what I have learned and felt during the first half of my graduate studies. In terms of design, paper selection and binding, the booklet avoids "formality" and is read in the exhibition hall as if one were flipping through a newspaper, trying to review and record one's learning in a relaxed and comfortable way.
